Formal Policy Statement

Wellen Quality Policy

Reviewed annually by senior management and upheld across all operations, contracts, and engagements.

Policy Statement

Wellen Specialists is committed to providing professional, reliable, and compliant Operations & Maintenance services for Sewage Treatment Plants that consistently meet or exceed the expectations of our clients and satisfy all applicable statutory, regulatory, and environmental requirements.

We shall achieve this by establishing measurable quality objectives across all our service lines, communicating them clearly to our team, and reviewing them at regular intervals to ensure continued relevance, adequacy, and effectiveness.

We are committed to the continual improvement of our Quality Management System, to the elimination of non-conformities at source, and to fostering a culture in which every member of our team takes personal ownership of the quality of their work.

Our Quality Pillars

Regulatory Compliance

Strict adherence to CPCB, SPCB, and BIS standards across every site. Zero tolerance for non-compliance events under our management.

Competent People

Regular technical training, certification, and performance review for all operators and engineers deployed at client sites.

Continual Improvement

Systematic root cause analysis for every incident, monthly KPI reviews, and a structured corrective action process across all contracts.

Client Satisfaction

Proactive communication, transparent monthly reporting, and swift response to any client concern — always measured, always on record.

Environmental Responsibility

Minimising chemical waste, water losses, and energy consumption at every plant we operate, consistent with our environmental commitment.

Documented Processes

All standard operating procedures, checklists, and maintenance schedules are documented, version-controlled, and accessible to site teams.
